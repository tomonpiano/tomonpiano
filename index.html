Tom On Piano

Tom is a pianist, composer and educator. He has performed on stage in London’s West End, in numerous shows at Edinburgh’s Fringe Festival and has appeared on TV and BBC radio. He’s held numerous residencies at international hotels and was principle piano player for shows, weddings and events at Disney Cruise Line from 2012 to 2018. Currently residing in Scotland, Tom is now focusing on writing, producing and teaching music in addition to his performing work around the country. Thanks for visiting!

Tom is a highly experienced wedding pianist having performed at hundreds of ceremonies and events in his career. He has a vast repertoire spanning classical, jazz, rock, pop and blues. Below is a video playlist demonstrating Tom's performing skills.
